“My dream for the future is to create my very own video game. Something that brings people together, tells a story worth remembering, and allows players to fully immerse themselves in a world I've imagined and built from the ground up. I envision a game that not only entertains but resonates emotionally, with meaningful characters, new creatures, and interesting environments. In addition to game development, I hope to explore the world of animation and voice acting. Animation excites me because it’s a powerful way to breathe life into the worlds that I’ve envisioned since I was a child. I’ve always wanted to give shape and motion to what once only lived only in my mind. Voice acting on the other hand, allows for the expression of emotion and personality in a unique, intimate way, it's the heart and soul behind the characters that players grow attached to. Ultimately, my goal is to be a storyteller across multiple mediums, blending technology and art to create experiences that leave a lasting impact. Whether through pixels, voices, or moving frames, I want to share stories that inspire others the way I've been inspired by games and animation throughout my life.”
